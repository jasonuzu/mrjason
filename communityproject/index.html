🎯 Community Project Holiday Tasks

社区设计假期任务清单
Progress: 0%
1
Choose Your Community Project Topic
选择你的社区设计主题
Identify a community need that you're passionate about. Think about: • What problems do you see in your community? • What change would you like to create? • How can you help others?
确定一个你关心的社区需求。思考： • 你在社区中看到什么问题？ • 你想创造什么改变？ • 你如何帮助他人？
2
Review the Assessment Criteria
澄清社区设计评估标准
Study pages 22-26 in your student handbook. Understand how you'll be assessed on: • Investigating (Criterion A) • Planning (Criterion B) • Taking Action (Criterion C) • Reflecting (Criterion D)
学习学生手册第22-26页。了解你将如何被评估： • 调查（标准A） • 规划（标准B） • 采取行动（标准C） • 反思（标准D）
3
Investigate If Your Service Action is an Authentic Need
调查你的服务行动是否真实需要
Research to prove your community need is real. You can: • Create and distribute a questionnaire (online or paper) • Interview community members • Research online for data and statistics • Take photos of the problem you've observed
研究证明你的社区需求是真实的。你可以： • 创建和分发问卷（在线或纸质） • 采访社区成员 • 在线研究数据和统计 • 拍摄你观察到的问题的照片
4
Start Your Process Journal
开始你的过程日志
Using the template provided, write AT LEAST ONE detailed entry. Include: • What research/investigation you've done • What you've learned about your topic • Your initial ideas and plans • Which ATL skills you've used • Evidence (photos, survey results, etc.)
使用提供的模板，至少写一篇详细的日志。包括： • 你做了什么研究/调查 • 你对主题学到了什么 • 你的初步想法和计划 • 你使用了哪些ATL技能 • 证据（照片、问卷结果等）
5
Define Your Goal and Global Context
确定你的目标和全球背景
Write a clear goal statement: "I will [goal type] in order to [how you'll make a difference]"
Choose ONE global context that connects to your project.
写一个清晰的目标陈述："我将[目标类型]以便[你将如何产生影响]"
选择一个与你的项目相关的全球背景。
6
Schedule Your Second Meeting with Supervisor
安排与督导员的第二次会议
Contact your supervisor to arrange the next meeting date. Be prepared to share your investigation results and initial ideas.
联系你的督导员安排下次会议日期。 准备分享你的调查结果和初步想法。

⚠️ Important Reminders | 重要提醒

✓ Keep ALL evidence (photos, survey results, interviews, research notes) - you'll need this for your report!

✓ 保留所有证据（照片、问卷结果、采访、研究笔记）- 你的报告需要这些！

✓ Be honest in your process journal - challenges and mistakes are valuable learning!

✓ 在过程日志中诚实记录 - 挑战和错误都是宝贵的学习！

✓ Don't wait until the last minute - start investigating NOW!

✓ 不要等到最后一刻 - 现在就开始调查！

💡 Pro Tip | 专业提示: The more thorough your investigation during the holiday, the easier your project will be later. Take time to do quality research now!
假期期间调查越彻底，以后的项目就越容易。现在花时间做好质量研究！
