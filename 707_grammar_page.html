Grammar Resources

Grammar forms the foundation of language learning. Understanding grammar rules helps you construct sentences correctly and communicate with confidence. Our grammar resources are designed to explain complex concepts in a clear, accessible way, with plenty of examples and practice opportunities. Explore our collection below to strengthen your English grammar knowledge.

Featured Grammar Topics

Present Tenses

Learn how to use present simple, present continuous, present perfect, and present perfect continuous tenses correctly.

She works in London. (Present Simple)
They are studying now. (Present Continuous)
Learn More

Modal Verbs

Understand how to use modals like can, could, may, might, must, should, and would to express ability, possibility, permission, and obligation.

You should see a doctor.
They might come to the party.
Learn More

Conditionals

Master the four types of conditional sentences to express real and unreal situations, hypothetical scenarios, and regrets.

If it rains, I will stay home. (First Conditional)
If I had more time, I would learn Japanese. (Second Conditional)
Learn More
